Governance

Our cooperative is governed democratically by its members. Meet the elected Board of Directors and learn how decisions are made transparently.

Democratic Structure

How We Are Governed

👥

General Body

All members form the governing authority. Meets annually (AGBM) before 30 September. Elects the board every 5 years and approves major decisions.

↓
🏛️

Board of Directors

12 elected directors manage day-to-day affairs. Elected by the General Body for a 5-year term. Guided by the cooperative's bylaws.
