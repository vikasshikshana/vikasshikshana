Merit Scholarships

Rewarding Academic Excellence

Our flagship scholarship program rewards academic excellence among children of members. Every year, we award merit scholarships to students who score 85% or above in SSLC (10th) and PUC (12th) examinations, as well as need-based scholarships for higher education.

Application Process

  1. 1
    Submit application with mark sheet
  2. 2
    Verification by Education Committee
  3. 3
    Interview (for higher education)
  4. 4
    Award ceremony at Annual Day

Program Details

SSLC Merit Scholarship₹5,000 – ₹15,000
PUC Merit Scholarship₹8,000 – ₹25,000
Higher Education ScholarshipUp to ₹50,000/year
EligibilityChildren of active members (min. 1 year)
Applications OpenJune – August each year
Students Benefited (2024)120 students

Digital Literacy

Bridging the Digital Divide

Our Digital Literacy Center, equipped with 50 computers and high-speed internet, offers computer literacy programs aligned with the Pradhan Mantri Gramin Digital Saksharta Abhiyan (PMGDISHA). We focus on empowering rural members, women, and senior citizens with essential digital skills.

Program Topics

  • Computer basics and internet usage
  • Google Search and email management
  • Digital payments (UPI, Net Banking)
  • Government e-services (Aadhaar, DigiLocker)
  • Online shopping and safety
  • Video calling and social media basics

Program Details

Computer Lab Capacity50 workstations
Internet Speed100 Mbps fiber optic
Course Duration20 hours (PMGDISHA)
FeeFree for members

Adult Education

Learning Has No Age

Our adult education programs target illiterate and semi-literate members, with special focus on women in rural areas. Programs include functional literacy, financial literacy, health awareness, and legal rights education — all conducted in Kannada.

Program Topics

  • Kannada reading and writing basics
  • Basic arithmetic and numeracy
  • Financial literacy (banking, savings, insurance)
  • Legal rights awareness (RTE, POCSO, RTI)
  • Health and nutrition education
  • Environmental awareness

Program Details

School Support

Supporting Government School Children

We actively support children of members studying in government schools through school supply distribution, free coaching centers, library book drives, and SSLC/CET preparation workshops. Every child deserves an equal opportunity to excel.

Key Activities

  • Annual school supply kits (notebooks, pens, geometry box)
  • Uniform distribution for BPL member families
  • Free SSLC and CET coaching classes
  • Science and math olympiad preparation
  • Library book donation to school libraries
  • Tablet/laptop loan scheme for members' children
